FAUVISM

Early 20th. Century Characteristics: • Bold, Vibrant colors. • Simplified forms • Flatness and Two-Dimensionality • Subjective Expression • Independent color • Freedom • Artistic freedom and experimentation.

CUBISM

Early 20th century Transformed traditional notions of representation by breaking down objects and figures into geometric shapes and presenting multiple angles and viewpoints. • Geometric shapes • Interlocking planes • Mixed media • Reconsider perceptions of space and forms. • Abstraction • Broken mirror effect

Avant-garde art is a term that refers to innovative or experimental movements in art, typically associated with pushing the boundaries of artistic expression and challenging established norms.

    CHARACTERISTICS:

  • Innovation
  • Experimentation
  • Rejection of tradition
  • Provocation
  • Social of political commentary
